# Milestone 2 Baseline Decision

## Status
The current household baseline is implemented and honestly verified. It includes pantry, reserve-aware withdrawal, and house-ready versus house-not-ready meaning for `item/apple` and `item/flour`, with no new saved household-purpose state.

## What Milestone 2 Now Proves
- one explicit household authority can sit cleanly between personal and civic scope
- pantry plus reserve-aware withdrawal creates a real `mine now` versus `ours later` choice
- house-ready meaning makes the protected future pie set legible without direct pantry baking
- personal, household, and civic authorities remain explicit and intact

## What Still Feels Thin
- the household layer is still narrow and emotionally light
- most of its weight sits in withdraw, reserve-break, and rebuild moments
- it does not yet feel like a richer household routine or identity

## Strongest Reasons To Close Milestone 2 Now
- Milestone 2 already proves the required difference between `mine`, `ours`, and `town`
- the household trade-off is real, readable, and honestly verified in play
- scope, save, and authority boundaries are still unusually clean at this stop point
- adding another mechanic now would expand household breadth to solve thinness instead of closing the milestone

## Strongest Reasons To Add One More Step
- the pantry can still feel more like protected storage than lived shared life
- the current slice is stronger as a boundary proof than as a durable household identity proof
- one more narrow mechanic could deepen the emotional reason to preserve or rebuild the set

## Decision
Close Milestone 2 now.

## If One More Step Is Justified
None is justified in this pass. No additional Milestone 2 household mechanic is authorized.

## What Must Not Change Either Way
- `world_state.household.inventory` remains the only live household authority
- no direct pantry baking and no household `item/pie`
- no shared coins, no shared tree ownership, and no civic rewrite
- no saved readiness, purpose, debt, owner, or partner-claim fields
- no mixed personal, household, and civic write paths

## Recommendation
Freeze Milestone 2 at the current verified household baseline. It is narrow, honest, and sufficient as the first proof of shared household life without forcing broader household expansion.

## Final Rule
Close the household baseline while it is still clear.
