# Economy Constitution

## Purpose
The economy exists to turn work and judgment into better life. It is a meaning system before it is a balancing system.

## Permanent Rule
Money is fuel, not the goal. The economy is healthy when currency opens capability, beauty, freedom, resilience, and shared life rather than becoming the final prize.

## What Must Stay Tangible and Legible
- where goods come from
- how value is created
- what a price means
- what is being traded away
- what an asset changes over time
- what a civic contribution changes in the world

Early play should stay grounded in visible goods, visible coins, visible places of trade, and visible consequences.

## Productive Assets vs Consumption
- **Consumption** solves a current need or desire and is materially spent.
- **Productive assets** keep changing tomorrow by generating yield, capacity, convenience, or access.

The distinction must remain easy to feel. A child should be able to sense why eating the pie and buying the tree are different kinds of choice.

## Money, Goods, Assets, and Public Contribution
- Goods are where value is first made visible.
- Money is how players store and redirect that value.
- Assets are how players turn stored value into better futures.
- Public contribution is how private sacrifice becomes shared life.

These should reinforce each other, not compete as separate games.

## What To Avoid for a Long Time
- abstract finance layers detached from place
- passive number-go-up reward systems
- opaque market simulation
- debt-heavy progression
- tax systems
- speculation-first loops
- invisible deductions that remove value without legible cause

## Anti-Patterns
- rewards that appear without believable work, trade, or stewardship
- purchases that do not change life, access, beauty, or shared conditions
- late-game content that makes early craft and assets spiritually obsolete
- economy features that are legible only to adults and hidden from children
- systems that make the player care about money more than what money can build
